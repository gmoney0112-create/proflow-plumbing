Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2026

What we collect

When you request a quote or schedule service through our website, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Service address
  • Service type, issue description, urgency level, preferred date and time, and any notes you provide

How we use your information

We use the information you submit to respond to your quote request, schedule plumbing services, communicate with you about your appointment, and follow up to ensure your satisfaction.

Third-party tools

  • GoHighLevel (CRM) — used to receive and manage quote requests and communicate with customers via phone, email, and SMS.
  • GitHub Pages — used to host this website.

SMS messaging

By providing your phone number and submitting a quote request, you consent to receive SMS text messages from ProFlow Plumbing. These messages may include:

  • Quote confirmations and appointment scheduling
  • Service reminders and technician ETA updates
  • Responses to your inquiries

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier and plan.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any SMS message at any time. You will receive one confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

For help: Reply HELP to any SMS or contact us at info@proflowplumbing.com.

We do not sell your data

We do not sell your personal information — including your phone number or SMS consent — to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data security

Your information is transmitted securely to our CRM. We do not store personal data on this website itself.

Data requests

To request access to or deletion of your submitted data, contact us at info@proflowplumbing.com.
